We also use your information to ensure our Apps are working as intended, such as Analytics Data, Crash Reports, tracking outages or troubleshooting issues that you report to us. We use information to help improve the safety and reliability of our Apps and services. This includes detecting, preventing, and responding to fraud, abuse, security risks, and technical issues that could harm C APPS, C APPS users, or the public.
